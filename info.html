Info & Sources

How the numbers in this planner are calculated, where they come from, and what they don't include.

The big caveat: rack rates aren't what you'll pay

Every number here is a rack rate (Disney's published list price). Almost nobody actually pays rack — Disney runs room offers, AP discounts, free dining, and seasonal promos throughout the year, and authorized resellers like Undercover Tourist discount tickets year-round.

Treat this planner as the upper bound. For a real quote on a specific stay, click the "Get UCT quote" or "Compare on Undercover Tourist" links beside each result.

Hotel rates

Source: mousesavers.com 2026 rate charts.

Tax: Already included in every rate (12.5% standard, 13.5% at All-Star resorts).

How nightly totals are calculated

Mousesavers publishes rates in three day-of-week tiers per season:

  • Mon–Wed — Mon, Tue, Wed
  • Sun/Thu — Sun, Thu
  • Fri/Sat — Fri, Sat

The calculator looks up each night's exact tier rate and sums them. The total is the actual rack rate, not an average. Flat-rate seasons (Easter, holidays, etc.) have all three tiers equal.

Coverage

v1 has 3 resorts seeded: Disney's All-Star Movies (value), Wilderness Lodge (deluxe), and Riviera Resort (DVC). Adding more is a data-entry task — extend rates.js following the existing pattern.

Park tickets

Source: mousesavers.com 2026 regular ticket grid.

Tax: Prices include 6.5% Florida sales tax.

Age brackets

  • Adult = 10+
  • Child = 3–9
  • Under 3 = free (no ticket required)

Gate-purchase prices for 3+ day tickets are $21.30 more each than the online prices used here.

6-tier interpolation

Disney prices tickets by date using ~6 tiers. Mousesavers publishes only the lowest (Tier 0) and highest (Tier 5); the four tiers in between are linearly interpolated. The 2026 date-to-tier mapping is hand-built from public knowledge and editable in tickets.js (tierCalendar).

Verified against UCT for Nov 15, 2026: Tier 4 yields $2,854 vs UCT's $2,834 — within 1%.

UCT discount estimate

The "≈ $X via UCT" line applies a typical ~4% discount for an authorized reseller (Undercover Tourist). Their actual quote varies by dates and current promos. Click "Get UCT quote" beside each ticket option for the real number. The UCT estimate is informational only — the trip total uses the Disney rack rate.

Dining

Sources: minitravellers.co.uk 2026 character dining guide and disneyfoodblog.com.

Tax & gratuity: Per-row prices are the prix-fixe menu price — pre-tax, pre-gratuity. The two toggles in the dining panel ("Include 6.5% sales tax" and "Include 20% gratuity") add those to the grand total.

Children under 3 dine free.

DVC (Disney Vacation Club) equivalent

For DVC resorts, the planner shows a "DVC equivalent" cost — what the same stay would cost if you rented points from a member instead of paying cash.

Two methods

  • Exact — when a points chart is loaded for the room, the planner multiplies the points needed for your dates by an estimated David's DVC Rental Store renter rate ($20–24/pt).
  • Estimate (~40% off cash) — when no points chart is loaded for that room, the planner approximates DVC cost as 40% off the cash rate. Real savings range 30–50% depending on resort and dates; 40% is the industry midpoint.

For an exact quote, go to dvcrequest.com.

Out of v1 scope

  • The other ~25 WDW Disney-owned hotels (only 3 are seeded so far)
  • Transportation, souvenirs, parking, MagicBand+, etc.
  • Discount/promo overlays (free dining, AP discounts, room offers) — these change month-to-month and would need a live data feed
